void Shell::MediaRendererModel::activateRenderer ( QModelIndex const &  index)

Marks the Multimedia::Renderer under the passed QModelIndex as active. Active means in this case that the renderer is used for playback. If the index is invalid or not Multimedia::Renderer was found for that index nothing happens.
